静岡大学にてアルベルト・アインシュタイン医科大学のJinshil Hyun先生の講演が行われ、その後研究室に訪問していただきました。

講演タイトル：
Cognitive Aging in Context: Integrating Real-World Psychosocial Experience and Passive Sensing Data
実世界データで読み解く認知老化：パッシブセンシングと心理社会的体験の統合

講師：Jinshil Hyun先生、Albert Einstein College of Medicine
https://einsteinmed.edu/faculty/15867/jinshil-hyun

概要：
　Population aging is increasing the burden of Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias (ADRD) and cognitive impairment. Yet an estimated 45% of dementia risk may be attributable to potentially modifiable factors. Advancing prevention and intervention requires two complementary research priorities: (1) early detection of subtle cognitive decline, and (2) a contextual understanding of how environmental and psychosocial conditions shape cognitive aging trajectories. Recent advances in mobile technology, smartphone-based assessments, and passive sensing make it increasingly feasible to measure cognitive function, daily experiences, and real-world exposures at scale and with high temporal resolution.
　In this talk, I will present an integrated, real-world data framework with three goals: (1) demonstrating the utility of ecological momentary assessment (EMA)–based measures of cognition to capture early ADRD risks; (2) characterizing individuals’ environmental contexts using Geographic Information System and GPS-derived mobility data; and (3) examining psychosocial and behavioral mechanisms underlying ADRD risks. Together, these approaches illustrate how data collected from multiple sources over time can strengthen our ability to identify early risk signals and target modifiable pathways to support healthy cognitive aging.
